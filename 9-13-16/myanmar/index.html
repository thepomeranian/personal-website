Quick History


1200 - 1800

  • 1287 - Conquered by the Mongols
  • 1531 - Portuguese help Myanmar separate from Mongols and reunites country as Burma
  • 1824-1826 - War with British India
  • 1852 - British annexes lower Burma
  • 1885-1886 - Bruma becomes a province of India

1900s - early 2000's

  • 1937 - Britain separates Bruma from India
  • 1942 - Japan invades and occupies Bruma
  • 1945 - Britain liberates Burma from Japan
  • 1948-1982 - Burma becomes independent under military rule and turns to socialism
  • 1987 - 1989 - Riots and repression causes many protests for a new government
  • 1990 - 1997 - Strive towards independence and democracy
  • 1997 - Admitted to Association of South East Asian Nations
  • 1998 - 2009 Constant battle for human rights, new rule, democracy, trade, sanctions, neighboring countries all urge Myanmar for change

    • 2009-Current

    US, China, Russia, and other major countries offered to help Myanmar if they agree to stop killing people and have more reforms
